# System SDK

Rules systems declare:

- `system.manifest.json`
- actor JSON schema
- item JSON schema
- sheet registrations
- dice formula registrations
- character creation templates
- character origin catalogs and create-time origin application where supported by the rules runtime
- character import normalizers
- compendium entries for system-specific items, spells, talents, gear, clues, rituals, and conditions
- condition and compendium action automation for actor quick rolls
- action-use consumption for spell slots, resources, strain, and consumable quantities
- equipment purchasing and currency updates where supported by the rules runtime
- guided advancement options for leveling or rank progression
- rest and recovery automation for pools, resources, spell slots, and rest-clearing conditions
- encounter threat catalogs and budgeted encounter planning
- monster actor data builders and action quick rolls where supported by the rules runtime
- optional server hooks

See `plugins/example-system-generic-fantasy`, `plugins/example-system-stellar-frontiers`, and `plugins/example-system-mystic-noir` for minimal installable systems. The primary built-in rules runtime is `dnd-5e-srd`, targeting SRD 5.2.1 / 5.5e-compatible play with Fighter, Barbarian, Bard, Cleric, Paladin, Druid, Wizard, and Rogue templates, SRD character origin catalogs, SRD-oriented character import, level advancement, hit dice, spell slots, saving throw quick rolls, class save proficiencies, skill and tool check quick rolls, class/template/background skill and tool proficiencies, SRD currency and equipment purchasing, computed Armor Class from SRD armor/shield items, Fighter Second Wind, Action Surge, Tactical Mind, Extra Attack, and Tactical Shift class-feature automation, Barbarian Rage, Rage Damage, Danger Sense, Reckless Attack, Extra Attack, and Fast Movement automation, Bard Bardic Inspiration, Jack of All Trades, Font of Inspiration, and Charisma spellcasting automation, Cleric Channel Divinity, Divine Spark, Turn Undead, and Sear Undead class-feature automation, Paladin Lay On Hands, Divine Smite, Paladin's Smite, Extra Attack, Faithful Steed, half-caster spell-slot, and Charisma spellcasting automation, Druid Wild Shape, Wild Companion, Wild Resurgence, full-caster spell-slot, and Wisdom spellcasting automation, Wizard Arcane Recovery rest automation, Rogue Sneak Attack and Cunning Strike class-feature automation, rest recovery, SRD monster stat blocks with CR/XP values, monster actor data derived from threat stat blocks, monster action quick rolls, 2024 Basic Rules XP encounter budgets, and compendium-backed Longsword, Dagger, Quarterstaff, Shortbow, Spear, Shield gear, Leather Armor, Chain Mail, Calligrapher's Supplies, Thieves' Tools, Healing Word, Fire Bolt, Cure Wounds, Shield spell, Chromatic Orb, Ice Knife, Ray of Sickness, Divine Smite, Alert, Magic Initiate, Savage Attacker, Blessed, Poisoned, and Restrained entries. Fighter Second Wind exposes `feature-second-wind-healing` as `1d10 + Fighter level`, spends one `secondWind` resource use, applies self-healing through the normal target-effect path, regains one expended use on a Short Rest, and regains all expended uses on a Long Rest. Fighter level 2 adds `feature-action-surge` as a stateful `actionSurge` resource use and `feature-tactical-mind-bonus` as a `1d10` bonus that spends one Second Wind use. Fighter level 5 adds Extra Attack metadata to weapon damage quick rolls and Tactical Shift metadata to Second Wind. Barbarian level 1 adds `feature-rage` as a stateful `rage` resource use plus `feature-rage-damage-bonus` as fixed bonus damage; Barbarian level 2 adds Danger Sense metadata to Dexterity saves and `feature-reckless-attack`; Barbarian level 5 adds Extra Attack and Fast Movement metadata. Cleric level 2 adds `feature-divine-spark-healing`, `feature-divine-spark-damage`, and `feature-turn-undead` quick rolls backed by the `channelDivinity` resource; Cleric level 5 adds `feature-sear-undead-damage` as Wisdom-modifier d8 radiant damage metadata attached to Turn Undead failed saves. Wizard level 1 adds `arcaneRecovery` as a once-per-Long-Rest resource that can recover selected expended spell slots on Short Rest up to half Wizard level rounded up, with no 6th-level-or-higher recovery. Bard level 1 adds `feature-bardic-inspiration` as a stateful Charisma-based resource action; Bard level 2 adds Jack of All Trades metadata to unproficient skill checks; Bard level 5 adds `feature-font-of-inspiration` and short-rest Bardic Inspiration recovery. Paladin level 1 adds `feature-lay-on-hands-healing` as variable point-pool healing; Paladin level 2 adds `feature-divine-smite-damage` with spell-slot upcasting or Paladin's Smite free-resource consumption; Paladin level 5 adds Extra Attack metadata and `feature-faithful-steed`. Druid level 2 adds `feature-wild-shape` and `feature-wild-companion` with Wild Shape or spell-slot consumption; Druid level 5 adds Wild Resurgence actions to restore Wild Shape from a spell slot or restore one level-1 spell slot from Wild Shape once per Long Rest. Rogue level 1 adds `feature-sneak-attack-damage` as scaling d6 damage with eligibility metadata; Rogue level 5 adds `feature-cunning-strike` and Cunning Strike metadata with Poison, Trip, and Withdraw rider options plus the Rogue save DC. Short Rests restore one expended Second Wind, one expended Rage, one expended Channel Divinity use, one expended Wild Shape use, and all expended Bardic Inspiration uses after Font of Inspiration is unlocked; Long Rests restore all uses, including Paladin Lay On Hands, Paladin's Smite, Faithful Steed, Druid Wild Shape, Wild Resurgence, half-caster spell slots, and Druid full-caster spell slots. Longsword, Dagger, Quarterstaff, Shortbow, Spear, Healing Word, Fire Bolt, Cure Wounds, Shield, Chromatic Orb, Ice Knife, Ray of Sickness, Divine Smite, and supported monster stat block actions produce actor-aware action formulas when attached to a sheet; SRD Healing Word, Cure Wounds, Chromatic Orb, Ray of Sickness, Ice Knife secondary damage, and Divine Smite also support `spellSlotLevel` upcasting formulas and matching spell-slot consumption. The older Generic Fantasy, Stellar Frontiers, and Mystic Noir runtimes remain available as demo systems for later expansion.

API callers can install a campaign's active system, list character templates, list D&D SRD character origin choices, build actors from those templates with starting items and currency, apply D&D SRD background/species selections during character creation, import normalized character data, create D&D SRD monster actors from threat stat blocks, list a system compendium, add item/spell/gear/talent/clue/ritual entries to actors, purchase SRD equipment with actor currency, apply or remove conditions, advance actors through the system's guided progression, apply short or long rest recovery, plan encounters from system threat budgets, optionally persist planned encounters, and retrieve actor sheets that include condition-aware ability/aptitude/skill quick rolls, SRD saving throw quick rolls with class proficiency bonuses, SRD skill and tool checks with proficiency/expertise bonuses, SRD class-feature quick rolls, SRD monster action quick rolls, compendium-backed action formulas, computed D&D SRD Armor Class details, and the system's inventory, spell, talent, clue, ritual, and resource surfaces. D&D SRD origin choices include Acolyte, Criminal, Sage, and Soldier backgrounds plus Dragonborn, Dwarf, Elf, Gnome, Goliath, Halfling, Human, Orc, and Tiefling species metadata. Create-time origin application sets background skills/tools, origin feat tracking, species traits/resources, starting 50 GP, and validated SRD ability-score increases. D&D SRD encounter planning now returns monster CR, XP, AC, HP, action summaries, party XP budgets, and a difficulty result based on low/standard/hard thresholds. Monster actor sheets expose attack and damage quick rolls such as `monster-scimitar-attack` and `monster-scimitar-damage` from stat block actions. The system purchase endpoint spends actor currency atomically with item creation, returns sheets with updated armorClass/armorClassDetails for armor and shield purchases, and rejects insufficient funds without mutating the actor. The system rest endpoint updates HP/strain/composure, hit dice, D&D Fighter Second Wind/Action Surge, Barbarian Rage, Bardic Inspiration/Font of Inspiration, Cleric Channel Divinity, Paladin Lay On Hands/Paladin's Smite/Faithful Steed, Druid Wild Shape/Wild Resurgence, and Wizard Arcane Recovery resources, other resource pools, spell slots where applicable, and rest-clearing conditions; D&D Arcane Recovery accepts an `arcaneRecovery` spell-slot selection on Short Rest and consumes the once-per-Long-Rest resource. The system roll endpoint can post action formulas into chat by roll id, including SRD `feature-*`, `monster-*`, `spell-*`, `item-*`, `skill-*`, `tool-*`, and `save-*` roll ids; level-5 Fighter, Barbarian, and Paladin weapon quick rolls include `metadata.attacksPerAction`, Second Wind includes Tactical Shift movement metadata when unlocked, Barbarian Rage/Reckless quick rolls include resource, damage-bonus, resistance, duration, advantage, and drawback metadata, level-2 Cleric Divine Spark/Turn Undead quick rolls include Channel Divinity resource and save DC metadata, level-5 Cleric Turn Undead metadata links to `feature-sear-undead-damage`, Bard Bardic Inspiration/Font quick rolls include die, duration, trigger, resource, and spell-slot metadata, Paladin Lay On Hands/Divine Smite/Faithful Steed quick rolls include point-pool, upcast, spell-slot/free-resource, and recovery metadata, Druid Wild Shape/Wild Companion/Wild Resurgence quick rolls include Wild Shape, spell-slot, form, recovery, and spell-slot-restoration metadata, and Rogue Sneak Attack/Cunning Strike quick rolls include scaling damage, once-per-turn eligibility, rider options, and Rogue save DC metadata. `spellSlotLevel` can upcast supported leveled spell healing and damage formulas, `resourceAmount` can choose a supported resource amount such as Lay On Hands points, `useFreeResource` can choose a supported free feature resource such as Paladin's Smite or Wild Companion's Wild Shape spend, `consumeResources: true` spends the matching spell slot, actor resource, strain, or consumable quantity before the roll is posted, and `applyEffect: true` applies supported damage/healing totals to target actor pools with target edit permission.
