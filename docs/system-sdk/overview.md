# System SDK

Rules systems declare:

- `system.manifest.json`
- actor JSON schema
- item JSON schema
- sheet registrations
- dice formula registrations
- character creation templates
- character origin catalogs and create-time origin application where supported by the rules runtime
- character import normalizers
- compendium entries for system-specific items, spells, talents, gear, clues, rituals, and conditions
- condition and compendium action automation for actor quick rolls
- action-use consumption for spell slots, resources, strain, and consumable quantities
- equipment purchasing and currency updates where supported by the rules runtime
- guided advancement options for leveling or rank progression
- rest and recovery automation for pools, resources, spell slots, and rest-clearing conditions
- encounter threat catalogs and budgeted encounter planning
- monster actor data builders and action quick rolls where supported by the rules runtime
- optional server hooks

See `plugins/example-system-generic-fantasy`, `plugins/example-system-stellar-frontiers`, and `plugins/example-system-mystic-noir` for minimal installable systems. Fighter Second Wind exposes `feature-second-wind-healing` as `1d10 + Fighter level`, spends one `secondWind` resource use, applies self-healing through the normal target-effect path, regains one expended use on a Short Rest, and regains all expended uses on a Long Rest. Fighter level 2 adds `feature-action-surge` as a stateful `actionSurge` resource use and `feature-tactical-mind-bonus` as a `1d10` bonus that spends one Second Wind use. Fighter level 5 adds Extra Attack metadata to weapon damage quick rolls and Tactical Shift metadata to Second Wind. Barbarian level 1 adds `feature-rage` as a stateful `rage` resource use plus `feature-rage-damage-bonus` as fixed bonus damage; Barbarian level 2 adds Danger Sense metadata to Dexterity saves and `feature-reckless-attack`; Barbarian level 5 adds Extra Attack and Fast Movement metadata. Cleric level 2 adds `feature-divine-spark-healing`, `feature-divine-spark-damage`, and `feature-turn-undead` quick rolls backed by the `channelDivinity` resource; Cleric level 5 adds `feature-sear-undead-damage` as Wisdom-modifier d8 radiant damage metadata attached to Turn Undead failed saves. Wizard level 1 adds `arcaneRecovery` as a once-per-Long-Rest resource that can recover selected expended spell slots on Short Rest up to half Wizard level rounded up, with no 6th-level-or-higher recovery. Bard level 1 adds `feature-bardic-inspiration` as a stateful Charisma-based resource action; Bard level 2 adds Jack of All Trades metadata to unproficient skill checks; Bard level 5 adds `feature-font-of-inspiration` and short-rest Bardic Inspiration recovery. Paladin level 1 adds `feature-lay-on-hands-healing` as variable point-pool healing; Paladin level 2 adds `feature-divine-smite-damage` with spell-slot upcasting or Paladin's Smite free-resource consumption; Paladin level 5 adds Extra Attack metadata and `feature-faithful-steed`. Druid level 2 adds `feature-wild-shape` and `feature-wild-companion` with Wild Shape or spell-slot consumption; Druid level 5 adds Wild Resurgence actions to restore Wild Shape from a spell slot or restore one level-1 spell slot from Wild Shape once per Long Rest. Ranger level 1 adds `feature-hunters-mark-damage` backed by Favored Enemy free uses or spell-slot consumption; Ranger level 5 adds Extra Attack metadata to weapon damage rolls. Monk level 1 adds Martial Arts damage/weapon formulas and Unarmored Defense AC; Monk level 2 adds Focus-backed Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, Step of the Wind, Unarmored Movement, and Uncanny Metabolism; Monk level 3 adds Deflect Attacks; Monk level 5 adds Extra Attack metadata and Stunning Strike. Sorcerer level 1 adds `feature-innate-sorcery` as a Bonus Action resource use with spell save DC and spell attack advantage metadata; Sorcerer level 2 adds Font of Magic, `feature-convert-spell-slot-to-sorcery-points`, `feature-create-spell-slot`, and Metamagic quick rolls; Sorcerer level 5 adds Sorcerous Restoration short-rest Sorcery Point recovery. Warlock level 1 adds Eldritch Invocations and Pact Magic with Short Rest slot recovery; Warlock level 2 adds Magical Cunning as a once-per-Long-Rest half-slot recovery action; Warlock level 5 upgrades Pact Magic to level-3 slots and exposes invocation-count metadata. Rogue level 1 adds `feature-sneak-attack-damage` as scaling d6 damage with eligibility metadata; Rogue level 5 adds `feature-cunning-strike` and Cunning Strike metadata with Poison, Trip, and Withdraw rider options plus the Rogue save DC. Dragonborn actors expose Breath Weapon and Draconic Flight resources/quick rolls, Dwarf actors apply Dwarven Toughness HP and expose Stonecunning, Goliath actors expose Giant Ancestry and Large Form, and Orc actors expose Adrenaline Rush and Relentless Endurance. Short Rests restore one expended Adrenaline Rush, one expended Second Wind, one expended Rage, one expended Channel Divinity use, one expended Wild Shape use, all Monk Focus uses, all Warlock Pact Magic slots, Sorcerer Sorcery Points through Sorcerous Restoration, and all expended Bardic Inspiration uses after Font of Inspiration is unlocked; Long Rests restore all uses, including Breath Weapon, Draconic Flight, Stonecunning, Giant Ancestry, Large Form, Relentless Endurance, Paladin Lay On Hands, Paladin's Smite, Faithful Steed, Ranger Favored Enemy, Monk Uncanny Metabolism, Druid Wild Shape, Wild Resurgence, Sorcerer Innate Sorcery, Sorcery Points, Sorcerous Restoration, Warlock Magical Cunning and Pact Magic slots, half-caster spell slots, Druid full-caster spell slots, and Sorcerer full-caster spell slots. Longsword, Dagger, Quarterstaff, Shortbow, Longbow, Scimitar, Shortsword, Spear, Healing Word, Fire Bolt, Cure Wounds, Shield, Chromatic Orb, Ice Knife, Ray of Sickness, Sorcerous Burst, Eldritch Blast, Hex, Divine Smite, Hunter's Mark, Dissonant Whispers, Dragon's Breath, Mind Spike, Ensnaring Strike, Starry Wisp, and supported monster stat block actions produce actor-aware action formulas when attached to a sheet; SRD Healing Word, Cure Wounds, Chromatic Orb, Ray of Sickness, Ice Knife secondary damage, Divine Smite, Dissonant Whispers, Dragon's Breath, Mind Spike, and Ensnaring Strike also support `spellSlotLevel` upcasting formulas and matching spell-slot consumption. The older Generic Fantasy, Stellar Frontiers, and Mystic Noir runtimes remain available as demo systems for later expansion.

The D&D SRD compendium also includes the remaining SRD 5.2.1 added-spell entries Aura of Life, Charm Monster, Elementalism, Phantasmal Force, Power Word Heal, Searing Smite, Summon Dragon, Tsunami, and Vitriolic Sphere. Bard, Cleric, Druid, Sorcerer, and Wizard spell-slot defaults now use the SRD full-caster slot table through level 20; spell action formulas cover Phantasmal Force `2d8`, Searing Smite upcasts, Tsunami initial/secondary damage, and Vitriolic Sphere initial/upcast/secondary damage with matching slot consumption when a valid slot pool exists.

The D&D SRD core spell slice now includes Acid Arrow, Acid Splash, Aid, Bane, Bless, Burning Hands, Color Spray, Command, Guiding Bolt, Magic Missile, and Thunderwave. Cantrip formula resolution scales Acid Splash, Fire Bolt, Sorcerous Burst, Eldritch Blast, and Starry Wisp at actor levels 5, 11, and 17; leveled spell upcasting covers Acid Arrow initial/secondary damage, Aid numeric hit-point increases, Burning Hands, Guiding Bolt, Magic Missile dice-plus-constant scaling, and Thunderwave.

API callers can install a campaign's active system, list character templates, list D&D SRD character origin choices, build actors from those templates with starting items and currency, apply D&D SRD background/species selections during character creation, import normalized character data, create D&D SRD monster actors from threat stat blocks, list a system compendium, add item/spell/gear/talent/clue/ritual entries to actors, purchase SRD equipment with actor currency, apply or remove conditions, advance actors through the system's guided progression, apply short or long rest recovery, plan encounters from system threat budgets, optionally persist planned encounters, and retrieve actor sheets that include condition-aware ability/aptitude/skill quick rolls, SRD saving throw quick rolls with class proficiency bonuses, SRD skill and tool checks with proficiency/expertise bonuses, SRD class-feature quick rolls, SRD species trait quick rolls, SRD monster action quick rolls, compendium-backed action formulas, computed D&D SRD Armor Class details, and the system's inventory, spell, talent, clue, ritual, and resource surfaces. D&D SRD origin choices include Acolyte, Criminal, Sage, and Soldier backgrounds plus Dragonborn, Dwarf, Elf, Gnome, Goliath, Halfling, Human, Orc, and Tiefling species metadata. Create-time origin application sets background skills/tools, origin feat tracking, species traits/resources, starting 50 GP, and validated SRD ability-score increases. D&D SRD encounter planning now returns monster CR, XP, AC, HP, action summaries, party XP budgets, and a difficulty result based on low/standard/hard thresholds. Monster actor sheets expose attack and damage quick rolls such as `monster-scimitar-attack` and `monster-scimitar-damage` from stat block actions. The system purchase endpoint spends actor currency atomically with item creation, returns sheets with updated armorClass/armorClassDetails for armor and shield purchases, and rejects insufficient funds without mutating the actor. The system rest endpoint updates HP/strain/composure, hit dice, D&D Fighter Second Wind/Action Surge, Barbarian Rage, Bardic Inspiration/Font of Inspiration, Cleric Channel Divinity, Paladin Lay On Hands/Paladin's Smite/Faithful Steed, Ranger Favored Enemy, Monk Focus/Uncanny Metabolism, Sorcerer Sorcery Points/Sorcerous Restoration, Warlock Magical Cunning/Pact Magic, Druid Wild Shape/Wild Resurgence, Wizard Arcane Recovery, Dragonborn Breath Weapon/Draconic Flight, Dwarf Stonecunning, Goliath Giant Ancestry/Large Form, and Orc Adrenaline Rush/Relentless Endurance resources, other resource pools, spell slots where applicable, and rest-clearing conditions; D&D Arcane Recovery accepts an `arcaneRecovery` spell-slot selection on Short Rest and consumes the once-per-Long-Rest resource.
